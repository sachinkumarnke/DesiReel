Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners. Cookies help provide a better and more personalized user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

DesiReel uses cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the DesiReel Service to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the DesiReel Service by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve the way our website works.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies These cookies are temporary and expire once you close your browser. Session
Persistent Cookies These cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. Up to 2 years
Authentication Cookies These cookies help us identify users so you don't need to log in each time you visit our site. 30 days
Preference Cookies These cookies remember your settings and preferences. 1 year
Analytics Cookies These cookies collect information about how you use our website. Up to 2 years

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the DesiReel Service and deliver advertisements on and through the DesiReel Service. These third parties may include:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Advertising partners
  • Payment processors

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience, since it will no longer be personalized to you. It may also stop you from saving customized settings like login information.

To manage cookies in different browsers, please refer to the following links:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at privacy@desireel.com.
