About DesiReel

Your ultimate destination for Indian entertainment

Our Story

Founded in 2020, DesiReel was born out of a passion to bring quality Indian entertainment to audiences worldwide. What started as a small streaming service with a handful of Bollywood titles has now grown into a comprehensive platform featuring thousands of movies and shows from across India.

Our mission is to showcase the rich diversity of Indian cinema and television, from mainstream Bollywood blockbusters to critically acclaimed regional content, making it accessible to viewers around the globe.

Today, DesiReel is proud to be one of the fastest-growing streaming platforms dedicated to Indian content, serving millions of users across multiple countries and continents.

What We Offer

Diverse Content

From Bollywood blockbusters to South Indian cinema, regional gems, and exclusive web series, we offer a wide range of content to cater to all tastes.

Global Access

Our platform is available worldwide, allowing the Indian diaspora and international audiences to enjoy quality Indian entertainment wherever they are.

Premium Experience

We provide high-definition streaming, multiple language subtitles, and a user-friendly interface across all devices for the best viewing experience.

Innovation
