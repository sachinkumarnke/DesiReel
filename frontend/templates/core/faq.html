Subscription & Billing

DesiReel offers several subscription plans to meet your needs. Our Basic plan starts at ₹199/month, Standard at ₹499/month, and Premium at ₹799/month. Each plan offers different features including video quality, number of screens, and download options.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. To cancel, go to your Account Settings and select "Cancel Subscription."

Content & Streaming

DesiReel offers a wide variety of Indian content including Bollywood movies, South Indian films (Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada), regional cinema (Bengali, Marathi, Punjabi), and exclusive web series. We regularly update our library with new releases and classics.

Yes, with our Standard and Premium plans, you can download movies and shows to watch offline. Simply look for the download icon on compatible titles and tap it to save the content to your device.

Technical Support

DesiReel is available on a wide range of devices including smartphones (iOS and Android), tablets, smart TVs, web browsers, and streaming devices like Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and Chromecast.

If you're experiencing streaming issues, try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Restart your device
3. Clear your browser cache or app data
4. Try a different browser or device
5. Contact our support team if the issue persists
